About Babylangues:

Founded in 2008, Babylangues is France’s original specialist in early language learning. We have developed a unique method for teaching English, focusing on play and creativity in the home setting. Our approach aims to make learning a language as natural and enjoyable as possible, mirroring the way children acquire their native language.

At Babylangues, we offer a distinctive experience in teaching English in France, seamlessly integrating English into the lives of French families and children.
